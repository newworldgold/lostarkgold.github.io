Lost Ark: How to get and use a Powerpass?

Lost Ark Date: Dec/10/21 14:03:34 Views: 129

 

Players will come across Lost Ark Powerpass from the start of the game. It may sound not very easy, and however, in reality, it's a pretty straightforward concept. In our guide, we'll share with you what is Powerpass, how to get it, and, in turn, use it.

Lost Ark Powerpass: What is it?

The whole point of Powerpass is that during this beta, if you finish the main story with one character, they give you a Powerpass, which is a mechanic that will level up your second character immediately to level 50 and give it level-appropriate gear. This helps players try the end game with another class to decide which playstyle they like better and perhaps lock in onto which class they'll play when the game finally launches.

What does the PowerPass give you?

If you activate it on one of your characters, the character immediately jumps to level 50 and receives equipment corresponding to its level. You can then jump directly into the endgame content with a newly created character.

You can only use the pass once per account, so think twice about which character you want to use it for.

How to get a Powerpass in Lost Ark?

If you're looking to experiment with all of Arkium's different heroes, the Powerpass will be your best friend. How do you get one, though?

    1). You must complete the Main Quest (Ealyn's Gift.)
    2). Check your in-game mail.
    3). You will now have a Powerpass, which a new character can use.

How to use Lost Ark's Powerpass?

Once you've obtained this elusive item, you'll want to make full use of it. To do so:

    1). Select the character you want to boost.
    2). Click the yellow button at the bottom of your screen.
    3). Use your Powerpass.
    4). You will then have to complete the "Adventurer's Path" quest, which encompasses levels 10 – 15.
    5). Completing this will equip your character with all the gear needed for the Vern content.

How long do you need to level 50?

Whether you can take advantage of the Power Pass as a new player. Because if you stumble through the world inexperienced, it will take a while before you can complete the story.

However, experienced players have already proven that they can complete the game's game's main story in about two days. So here, the power pass is within reach.

However, these players, in particular, usually already know how the characters feel in the endgame. So it may be that the Power Pass does not come to the aid of the players who need it.
